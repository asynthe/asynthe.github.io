ORG

attaching images

Displaying images on org is easy, you can add the image as

[[./image.png]] or [[~/path/image.png]]

Now, active the image display with M-x org-display-inline-images

CHANGING THE IMAGE SIZE add the next to your configuration: 

(setq org-image-actual-width nil)

now you can add something like #+ATTR_HTML: width="100px" #+ATTR_ORG: :width 100 [[./image.png]]

There are much more metadata for images, such as: #+CAPTION: A image of a dog #+ATTR_HTML: :alt dog :title dog representation :align right #+ATTR_HTML: :width 50% :height 50%
