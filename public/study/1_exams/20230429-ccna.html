STUDY MATERIAL

  • Chris Bryant's Cisco CCNA 200-301 course on Udemy
  • [BEST SOURCE] ExamHighPass for Cisco 200-301 CCNA. It comes with a practice exam, each with 425 questions (like the real exam). It's a great tool to test your knowledge and see where you stack up. Each question also contains very in-depth and comprehensive answers to re-enforce your understanding.
  • The CCNA 200-301 exam topics guide. Yes, this is very useful. You can view it on Cisco's website. It lists all the topics that you need to know for the exam. Make sure you understand each point and can explain it confidently.
