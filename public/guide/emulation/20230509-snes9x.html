usage

$ snes9x <flags> <path/to/file(.zip, .gzip, JMA or compress)>

flags

-ntsc -> ntsc video mode, 60hz -pal -> pal video mode, 50hz

-fullscreen -> -soundsync ->

VIDEO MODES -v1 -> Blocky (default) -v2 -> TV -v3 -> Smooth -v4 -> SuperEagle -v5 -> 2xSaI -v6 -> Super2xSaI -v7 -> EPX -v8 -> hq2x
