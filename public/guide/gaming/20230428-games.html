STARTING THE GAME Recommended Skills:

  • Lockpicking
  • Computer
  • Electronic
  • Weapons: Low-Tech

Stay away from this Skills:

Swimming is not useless, but you can use the rebreather item. Hazmat suits make environmental training redundant. Don't need to invest in demolition. Leveling up medicine is lost by regenerating orb got in half of game.

There are Augmentation Canisters throughout the game, with two abilities to choose from. USEFUL ABILITIES:

  • Health Regeneration
  • Microfibral Muscles
  • Cloak
  • Aggresive Defence System
  • Speed Enhancement
  • Power Recirculator

STAY AWAY FROM:

  • Aqualung
  • Run Silent
  • Environmental Resistance

STEALTH Baton, Riot Prod, Pepper Spray.

  • Try to hit enemies in lower pelvis.
  • Crouching and sneaking you can get as close as you like.
  • If alerted, just keep whacking them down.
  • Pepper Spray where you can't get behind the enemies.
  • Hit them with the Prod just once, then hit with Baton.
